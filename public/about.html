About Me

My professional journey has been marked by a dynamic blend of experiences, each contributing to my growth, adaptability, and skill development. From a young age, I began as a janitor, learning the importance of meticulous work and attention to detail. At Chick-fil-A, I honed my leadership skills during my tenure as a Team Lead, overseeing operations and fostering a culture of exceptional service.

Transitioning to an apprenticeship in commercial electrical work, I gained technical expertise in electrical systems, installation, and maintenance. Simultaneously, I ventured into landscaping management, contributing my skills during various seasons over four years. These experiences enriched my knowledge of grounds maintenance, project management, and horticultural practices.

My diverse background has instilled in me a strong work ethic, adaptability, and the ability to thrive in various work environments. I'm eager to leverage this multi-faceted skill set in new challenges and continue learning, growing, and contributing to future endeavors.

If you're seeking a dedicated and versatile professional or would like to connect to discuss opportunities, please don't hesitate to reach out. Let's collaborate and create a brighter future together.
